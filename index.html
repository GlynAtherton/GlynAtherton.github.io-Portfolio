About Me

Following many years working in IT as a UNIX systems administrator, I wanted to do something more interesting and enjoyable.

I trained and qualified as a drone pilot and started a side business providing aerial photography and videography. Fot this I decided to build my own website. I studied some Pluralsight courses to develop my skills with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

I thoroughly enjoyed the process of building my own website and so I decided to study hard to pursue a different career path doing something I believe I will really enjoy and be good at.

I invested in some more in depth courses with Codecademy and further developed my HTML, CSS and JavaScript skills along with Python, PHP, SQL, Java and C#. With my background of UNIX and Linux, I am well equipped for a new career as a Full Stack Developer.
